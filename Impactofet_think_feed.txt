## 📊 Business Impact of et_think_feed

### 🧠 Problem Framing
Professionals, students, and decision-makers spend significant time consuming fragmented news across multiple sources to understand a single topic. This results in:

- High time cost  
- Cognitive overload  
- Slower decision-making  

---

## ⏱️ 1. Time Saved

### 📌 Assumptions:
- Average user reads **5 articles/day**
- Average time per article = **5 minutes**
- Total time spent = **25 minutes/day**

With **et_think_feed**:
- 1 AI briefing takes ≈ **5 minutes**

### ✅ Impact:
- **Time saved = 20 minutes/day/user (~80%)**
- Monthly (20 working days) = **400 minutes (~6.7 hours)**

👉 For 1,000 users:
- **6,700 hours saved/month**

---

## 💰 2. Cost Reduction

### 📌 Assumptions:
- Average professional hourly value = **₹500/hour**

### ✅ Impact:
- 6.7 hours saved/month → **₹3,350 saved per user/month**

👉 For 1,000 users:
- **₹33,50,000/month (~₹33.5 Lakhs)** in productivity value

---

## 📈 3. Revenue / Opportunity Gain

### 📌 Assumptions:
- Faster insights improve decision-making quality by **~1%**

### ✅ Impact:

- **₹10,00,000/month additional value created**
